Pardonned · redesign exploration · May 2026

Two directions per page.

Restructured home, search, per-president, and detail pages — each in a "safe" version (close to the existing visual language) and a "bold" version (more editorial, larger type, more committed use of the red accent). All pages share the same DM Serif Display + DM Sans system and color tokens from your DESIGN.md.

What changed from the live site

  • By-administration comparison strip on the home page — the missing module for ranking presidents by grants and restitution.
  • Restitution leaderboard as a first-class home-page section — the dollar story is now the headline, not buried.
  • Per-president overview pages with category breakdown, monthly heatmap, and timeline of major actions.
  • Search: safe version cleans up filter density; bold version becomes a proper sortable data table with comparative columns.
  • Detail pages: a "known/unknown" stat strip surfaces money clearly; the warrant text gets pull-quote treatment so sparse cases still feel substantial.
  • Same color/type tokens throughout — pulled directly from your DESIGN.md.
