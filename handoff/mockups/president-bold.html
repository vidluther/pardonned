Donald J. Trump.Second term — 121 grants, $1.35B forgiven.

Trump's second-term clemency record is dominated by a single inauguration-day act: a blanket pardon for roughly 1,500 January 6 defendants. Subsequent grants have concentrated in financial fraud, cryptocurrency, and political cases — together totaling more restitution forgiven than the previous eight administrations combined.
